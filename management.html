Khaana — Management Team

Rustic, authentically Mexican hospitality — powered by a dual model: Restaurant + Food Truck Mariachi Thu–Sat 7pm

Meet the leadership behind Khaana—a neighborhood cocina blending tradición and smart operations. We run a full-service restaurant in parallel with a mobile food truck to reach festivals, campuses, and late-night crowds, while our live Mariachi (Thu–Sat, after 7pm) turns dinner into a celebration.

Our Leadership Values (Unordered List)

  • Respect for recipes, regions, and the people who taught us.
  • Operational excellence and safety across kitchen, truck, and stage.
  • Community ties—local sourcing and pop-ups with neighborhood partners.

90-Day Priorities (Ordered List)

  2. Launch seasonal antojitos menu aligned with supply and prep capacity.
  3. Formalize Mariachi schedule & setlist flow for table-turn balance post-7pm.
